About Us

Founded by Precious Radebe, our organization empowers domestic workers and gardeners with practical skills to succeed in the job market. We provide advanced training in cleaning, landscaping, life skills, and entrepreneurship to help workers secure better-paying jobs and opportunities. Our programs also benefit employers by supplying skilled, confident, and reliable staff.
